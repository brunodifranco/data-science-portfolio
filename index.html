    Solving business problems that are close to real problems at various companies, using Data Science skills and publicly available data. This solutions start with understanding the business problem, and end with deploying the final product by using a Cloud Computing tool.

    Undergraduate Final Paper: “Political News and Future Markets Returns”

    Exploring the impacts of political news in Brazilian future markets returns, more specifically in U.S. Dollar Futures (DOL), BOVESPA Index Futures (IND) and 1-Day Interbank Deposits Futures (DI1), using Python, Time Series modeling with GARCH and Webscraping Techniques. Despite common sense, the results suggest no significant impact of political news on the volatility series for all three assets, and hence no significant impact in their respective returns.

    Business solution for real estate company

    In this insights project I used Python and Streamlit to solve a profit maximization problem for House Rocket, a fictitious real estate company, by suggesting whether a property should or shouldn't be bought and resold.
